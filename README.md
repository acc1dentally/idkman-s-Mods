# idkman-s-Mods
A hub for all the mods I've made for various games. 


## Pillars of Eternity 2: Deadfire

**1. Sword - The Doom of Illyria**

   
      Despoiler of Illyria: 35% chance to recover instantly on attacking
   
        Upgrade: Conqueror of Illyria: 75% chance to recover instantly on attacking

      Arcane Surge: +3 to Metaphysics, Arcana. +25% action speed with spells

      Octarine Carved Pattern: +5% Crit chance, +5% weapon damage, -5% damage from spells (all scales with Metaphysics Skill)
   
  
**2. Pistol - Reqiuem**


      Veil-folded Magazine: Fires three shots before reloading, but reloads are slower. +25% Crit Chance

        Upgrade: Superenchanted Ammunition: Grants modal ability (Superenchanted Ammunition)

      Dazzling Shots: Hits with this weapon have a 40% chance to cast Arkemyr's Dazzling Lights

      Evelyn's Storm: Evelyn has found her birthright from beyond the wheel. 75% chance to cast Chain Lightning on Crit

     
**3. Cloak - Forgotten Caress**


      Cursed to Endure: Ability triggers when at 25% Health, cast Barring Death's Door

      Divine Embrace: +10 Health restored every 3 seconds, +5 Constitution

      Scent of Jasmin and Lilac: 15% of incoming Crits converted to Hits, +10 to all defenses except deflection (scales with Diplomacy)


## Baldur's Gate 3
**1. Longsword - Ivar's Last Trick**
 
      <<Gish>> Wizard King's Strength
        Toggled: Set your Strength to 14 (and up, scales with level) / You can cast spells as a bonus action once per turn.
      <<Rogue>> Rakish Superiority
        Toggled: Rakish Sneak Attack heals you / Rakish Sneak Attack can apply Paralyzed.
      <<Wizard>> Songs of Conquest
        Bladesong adds +1 Bladesong resource every 5 levels, +1 flat damage reduction.
      <<Bard>> Dueling Hearts
        If your off-hand is empty:
          Gain +1 Charisma, +1 Sleight of Hand.
          If you use a Blade Flourish (Melee), you can make one extra weapon attack this turn.
      UnlockSpell: Denoument!
 
**2. Longsword - Ivar's Last Hope**
 
      <<Gish>> Wizard King's Aegis
        Toggled: Gain +10 AC and +1 to Saving Throws / You can cast spells as a bonus action per turn
      <<Gish>> Sword and Spell
        Whenever you cast a spell, gain a +1d4 damage bonus to weapon attacks. Whenever you deal damage with a weapon, gain +1 to all spell damage rolls.
      <<Duelling>> Duelling Hearts
        If your off-hand is empty:
          Gain +1 to Charisma
          Gain Tactical Insight at the start of every turn. Tactical Insight: +1 to all attack rolls, -1 to Crit Threshold, resets on combat end.
          You can make a free attack after casting a spell.
      UnlockSpell: Arcane Infusion
 
**3. Ring - Midnight Mirage**
 
      Midnight Mirage: Add your Intelligence modifier to ability checks. Once per combat, if you get Downed, survive with 1 HP instead.
 
      Fools Journey: Give a +1 AC bonus to companions and nearby allies. Additionally, each companion with a 50m radius gains a unique bonus.
 
        Gale:
          Chosen of Mystra: Gain +1 to Spell Attack and Spellsave DC (increases with level)
          Karsite Conduit: Take 2d4 damage to immediately gain a level 3 spellslot (increases with level)
        Laezel:
          Gain Challenge to Duel action.
          Bodyguard: When an ally nearby is attacked, you can redirect the damage to yourself using a reaction.
        Shadowheart:
          Healslut: Whenever you heal an ally, also heal an additional +1d6 and give Blade Ward for 1 turn.
        Wyll:
          Fortune Favors the Blade: Whenever you roll a 1 on a rapier attack, roll again. While wielding a rapier, gain an AC bonus equal to your Charisma modifier.
        Karlach:
          Blind Wrath: While Raging, gain +2 to Attack Rolls and Resistance to Physical damage, but also gain Blinded.
        Minthara:
          Bindings of the Spider Queen: Gain the Subservient Drow condition. Players gain the Command Minthara bonus action.
        Jaheira:
          Storied Veteran: At the start of your turn, gain 2d4 Temp HP, gain +1 to All Saving Throws.
        Minsc:
          Buttkicker!: Deal +1d8 damage to enemies of evil alignment.
